# Learn Me — Example Questions

Use as inspiration, not scripts. Always personalize. Ordered from light to deep.

1. What's been the best part of your day so far?
2. What did you have for breakfast — or do you skip it?
3. How do you usually spend your evenings?
4. What's something small you genuinely look forward to every day?
5. Are you a morning person or a night owl?
6. What's the last thing you watched or listened to that you enjoyed?
7. What does a typical weekend look like for you?
8. Been anywhere interesting lately?
9. Do you have any pets?
10. What's something you've been meaning to try cooking lately?
11. How do you get around most days — driving, walking, transit?
12. Is there an app or site you check more often than you should?
13. Are you reading, watching, or playing anything that's got you hooked?
14. Have you picked up anything new recently — a habit, hobby, show?
15. What kind of music have you had on lately?
16. Is there a spot near you that you really like spending time?
17. What's the last thing that genuinely made you laugh?
18. Do you eat the same things most weeks, or like to mix it up?
19. When you have a free afternoon, what do you gravitate toward?
20. What's a meal that always hits right, no matter what kind of day?
21. Do you prefer working around people or do you need it quiet?
22. What kind of weather puts you in a good mood?
23. Is there a type of book, movie, or show you keep circling back to?
24. How do you like to celebrate good things — big or small?
25. What's a small indulgence you let yourself have?
26. When you need to recharge, do you go somewhere or stay put?
27. What's a topic you could talk about for way too long?
28. Do you like planning things out or figuring it out as you go?
29. What's something most people enjoy that doesn't do it for you?
30. How do you stay in touch with people you care about?
31. Is there a season that just feels like yours?
32. What's a skill you picked up just because you wanted to?
33. Do you like where you're living right now?
34. What's something you're a little particular about?
35. When you're having a rough day, what helps you reset?
36. What's a place you'd go back to in a heartbeat?
37. Do you keep up with the news, or try to keep distance from it?
38. What's a little routine you have that might seem quirky to others?
39. What's something you've changed your mind about recently?
40. How did you end up doing what you do now?
41. What's something you do differently from most people you know?
42. Is there a place that shaped who you are?
43. What do you wish more people understood about your day-to-day?
44. What does "enough" look like to you?
45. How do you decide whether to stick with something or walk away?
46. What's something you used to care about that doesn't matter as much now?
47. What's a lesson you learned the hard way?
48. What's something you've always been curious about but never explored?
49. Where would you like your life to be in a few years?
50. Who's someone whose way of living you admire?
51. What's a conversation that stuck with you recently?
52. How do you handle it when someone close to you sees things completely differently?
53. What's one thing from your upbringing you've held onto?
54. Is there a part of your work that challenges you in a way you enjoy?
55. What does a really good friendship look like to you in practice?
56. When facing a big decision, what's your process?
57. What's something you've built or created that you're quietly proud of?
58. Is there a question you wish people would stop asking you?
59. How do you go about learning something new?
60. What's an experience that shifted how you see things?
61. What's a story from your life you notice yourself telling again and again?
62. What's a belief you hold that's hard to explain to others?
63. When you think about people who've influenced you most, what do they have in common?
64. What's something you'd want to pass along to someone younger?
65. How has your idea of a good life changed over time?
66. Is there a line you won't cross, even when it would make things easier?
67. What's a part of who you are that took a while to fully own?
68. When you've gone through hard times, what kept you going?
69. What do you want to be remembered for by people who know you?
70. Does forgiveness come easily to you?
71. What's a responsibility you carry that most people don't see?
72. Is there something about the world that you can't make peace with?
73. What's the most important thing you've learned about yourself recently?
74. How do you know when you can really trust someone?
75. What's something you sacrificed that you'd do again?
76. What about yourself are you still working on?
77. What's a memory that comes back to you more often than people realize?
78. What's a fear that quietly shapes how you live?
79. What do you most want out of this life?
80. Is there a past version of yourself you're still making peace with?
81. What's something you need people to understand about you but struggle to say?
82. What's the bravest thing you've ever done that nobody noticed?
83. If you could go back and tell yourself one thing at 18, what would it be?
84. What's something you haven't done yet but would regret never trying?
85. When do you feel most like yourself?
86. You mentioned [X] the other day — what got you into that?
87. You brought up [person] — they sound important to you. What's that like?
88. You mentioned [experience] — how did that change things for you?
89. You said something about [opinion] — do you still feel that way?
90. You talked about [place] — what is it about that place that sticks with you?
91. You said you used to [activity] — do you miss it?
92. You mentioned [challenge] in passing — how's that going?
93. You brought up [goal] a while back — any movement on that?
94. (knows their work) What's the hardest part to explain to outsiders?
95. (knows their background) What do you think that taught you about yourself?
96. (knows a value) Was there a specific moment that made it matter to you?
97. (knows a hard experience) Can you trace something about who you are now back to that?
98. (knows a routine) What would it mean if you couldn't do that anymore?
99. (knows a close relationship) What do they see in you that you sometimes can't?
100. (knows a decision they face) What would it take to feel sure about it?
