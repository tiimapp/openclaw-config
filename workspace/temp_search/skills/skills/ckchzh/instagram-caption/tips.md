# Instagram Tips & Best Practices

## Algorithm Insights (2024-2025)
- **Reels > everything** for reach — Instagram is pushing short-form video hard
- **Saves and Shares** are the strongest engagement signals (more than likes)
- **Carousel posts** get re-shown to followers who didn't swipe all slides
- **First comment** with hashtags works just as well as inline hashtags
- **Consistency** matters more than frequency — 3-5 posts/week is ideal
- **Story engagement** (polls, questions, quizzes) boosts your main feed visibility

## Caption Structure
1. **Hook** (first line): Must be compelling — it's all people see before "more"
2. **Value** (body): Story, tip, or insight
3. **CTA** (end): Ask a question, "save this", "share with a friend"
4. **Hashtags** (after CTA or first comment): 20-30 relevant ones

## Hashtag Strategy (30 Tags)
- **10 Small** (<10K posts): Niche, specific, less competition
- **10 Medium** (10K-500K posts): Category-specific
- **10 Large** (500K+ posts): Broad but relevant
- Rotate hashtag sets (3-5 different sets) to avoid shadowban
- Research competitors' hashtags for inspiration

## Reels Best Practices
- **3-7 seconds** to hook the viewer
- **15-30 seconds** is the sweet spot for most content
- **Trending audio** boosts discovery
- **Text overlays** — many watch without sound
- **Loop trick** — make the end flow into the beginning
- **Pattern interrupt** every 2-3 seconds

## Story Ideas
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Polls and quizzes (boost engagement signals)
- "This or That" templates
- Day-in-the-life sequences
- Countdown to launches
- Q&A sessions

## Bio Optimization
- 150 characters max
- Line breaks make it scannable
- Include ONE clear CTA
- Use a link-in-bio tool for multiple links
- Category/niche keyword in Name field (searchable!)

## Content Pillars (Pick 3-5)
1. Educational (tips, how-tos, tutorials)
2. Entertaining (humor, trends, relatable)
3. Inspirational (quotes, transformations)
4. Personal (behind-the-scenes, stories)
5. Promotional (products, services, launches)
