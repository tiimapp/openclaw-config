# LinkedIn Tips & Best Practices

## Algorithm Insights (2024-2025)
- **Dwell time matters**: Longer posts that keep people reading get boosted
- **First 3 lines are everything**: The "see more" hook determines engagement
- **Comments > Reactions**: Posts with comments rank higher than those with just likes
- **Carousel posts** get 1.5-2x more reach than text-only posts
- **Avoid external links** in the post body — put them in comments instead
- **Post timing**: Tue-Thu, 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM in your audience's timezone
- **Engagement window**: Reply to every comment within the first hour

## Post Structure That Works
1. **Hook** (line 1-3): Stop the scroll
2. **Story/Context** (body): Build tension or teach
3. **Insight** (key takeaway): The "aha" moment
4. **CTA** (last line): Ask a question or invite action

## Hook Formulas
- "I {did X}. Here's what happened."
- "Stop doing {common mistake}."
- "Nobody talks about {uncomfortable truth}."
- "{Number} lessons from {experience}."
- "I was wrong about {thing}. Here's why."

## Hashtag Strategy
- Use 3-5 hashtags (not 30 like Instagram)
- Mix: 1 broad (#Leadership) + 2 niche (#ProductManagement) + 1-2 branded
- Place hashtags at the END of the post, not inline

## Content Pillars (Pick 3-4)
1. Industry insights & trends
2. Personal stories & lessons
3. How-to / educational content
4. Behind-the-scenes / day-in-life
5. Contrarian takes / hot takes
6. Celebrating others / shoutouts

## What NOT to Do
- Don't start with "I'm excited to announce..."
- Don't use corporate jargon without adding value
- Don't post and ghost — engage with commenters
- Don't repost without adding your perspective
- Don't use engagement bait ("Like if you agree!")
