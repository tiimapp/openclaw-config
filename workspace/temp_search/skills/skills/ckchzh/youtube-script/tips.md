# YouTube Tips & Best Practices

## Algorithm Insights (2024-2025)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** and **Watch Time** are the two most important metrics
- **Average View Duration (AVD)** — aim for 50%+ retention
- **First 30 seconds** determine if viewers stay or bounce
- **Thumbnails** drive CTR more than titles
- **Shorts** feed into long-form subscriber growth
- **Community posts** boost channel engagement signals

## Script Structure (The YouTube Formula)
1. **HOOK** (0-30s): Pattern interrupt, bold claim, or question
2. **INTRO** (30s-1min): What they'll learn, why it matters
3. **BODY** (bulk): Content with re-hooks every 2-3 minutes
4. **CLIMAX**: The biggest insight or payoff
5. **CTA** (end): Subscribe, comment, next video

## Title Formulas That Work
- "How I [Result] in [Timeframe]"
- "[Number] [Things] That [Benefit]"
- "Why [Common Thing] Is [Surprising Truth]"
- "I Tried [Thing] for [Time] — Here's What Happened"
- "[Thing] Is Dead. Here's What's Next."
- "The [Adjective] Guide to [Topic]"

## Thumbnail Best Practices
- **3 words or less** on thumbnail text
- **High contrast** colors (yellow/red on dark backgrounds)
- **Face with emotion** increases CTR by 30%+
- **Before/After** layout for transformation content
- **Avoid clutter** — should be readable at mobile size (small)
- **Consistent branding** but not boring

## SEO Essentials
- **Title**: Primary keyword in first 60 characters
- **Description**: 200+ words, keywords in first 2 sentences, timestamps, links
- **Tags**: 5-10 relevant tags (mix broad + specific)
- **Filename**: Rename video file to include keyword before upload
- **Closed captions**: Upload SRT for accessibility + SEO boost

## Retention Hacks
- **Re-hooks**: "But here's where it gets interesting..." every 2-3 min
- **Pattern interrupts**: Change camera angle, add B-roll, use sound effects
- **Open loops**: Tease something coming later to keep viewers watching
- **Visual variety**: Switch between talking head, screen share, B-roll
- **Chapters**: Help viewers navigate and boost "time watched" signals

## Posting Strategy
- **Best days**: Tue, Thu, Sat
- **Best times**: 2-4 PM (audience timezone) — gives time to index before prime time
- **Consistency** > frequency — weekly is fine if you're reliable
- **First 24 hours**: Share everywhere, reply to every comment
