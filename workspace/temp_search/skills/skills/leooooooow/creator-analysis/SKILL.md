# Creator Analysis

Analyze creators with a short, decision-ready workflow.

## Workflow

1. Define the decision.
   - shortlist creators
   - compare two or more creators
   - diagnose why performance is weak
   - summarize a creator profile for outreach

2. Normalize the inputs.
   - creator handle / profile link
   - niche and product category
   - audience and offer constraints
   - any observed metrics, comments, or content samples

3. Score the creator on five dimensions.
   - audience relevance
   - content clarity
   - proof / trust signals
   - conversion potential
   - operational risk

4. Write the recommendation in plain language.
   - what this creator is good for
   - where the fit is weak
   - whether to test, prioritize, or skip

## Output format

Return:
- one-line verdict
- strengths
- risks
- recommended use case
- next action

Keep it concise and decision-oriented.

## Quality bar

- Prefer observable evidence over hype words.
- Separate reach from fit.
- Flag uncertainty when metrics are missing.
- Do not confuse polished content with conversion potential.
